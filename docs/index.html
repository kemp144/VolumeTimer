QuietLater

QuietLater is a calm Mac utility that lets you mute your Mac, set a target volume, fade the volume down, and optionally restore the previous volume later.

What It Does

  • Mute your Mac immediately or after a timer
  • Set system volume to a chosen percentage
  • Fade volume down during the countdown
  • Restore the previous volume after a delay
Support Privacy Policy

QuietLater works locally on your Mac and is designed to stay simple, focused, and private.
